---

### **General Tests:**
1. **Multicollinearity Test** (e.g., Variance Inflation Factor).  
2. **Model Specification Test** (e.g., Ramsey RESET).  
3. **Stationarity Tests** (e.g., Augmented Dickey-Fuller, Phillips-Perron).  
4. **Outlier and Influential Observation Tests** (e.g., Cook's Distance, Leverage).  

---

### **Model-Specific Tests:**

#### **Pooled OLS:**
1. **Heteroskedasticity Tests** (e.g., Breusch-Pagan, White).  
2. **Serial Correlation Tests** (e.g., Durbin-Watson, Breusch-Godfrey).  
3. **Cross-Sectional Dependence Tests** (e.g., Pesaran's CD, Breusch-Pagan LM).  
4. **Normality Test of Errors** (e.g., Shapiro-Wilk, Jarque-Bera).  

#### **Fixed Effects (FE):**
1. **F-Test for Fixed Effects**.  
2. **Serial Correlation Tests** (e.g., Durbin-Watson, Breusch-Godfrey).  
3. **Cross-Sectional Dependence Tests** (e.g., Pesaran's CD, Breusch-Pagan LM).  

#### **Random Effects (RE):**
1. **Hausman Test** (FE vs. RE).  
2. **Breusch-Pagan Lagrange Multiplier (LM) Test** (for RE necessity).  
3. **Heteroskedasticity Tests** (e.g., Breusch-Pagan, White).  
4. **Normality Test for Random Effects** (e.g., Shapiro-Wilk, Jarque-Bera).
